ABOUT US

We are The Everyday Sneaker Shoe, we have been producing and selling our shoes here in Ikeja since 2005.

We believe in only using the best material, manufacturing them in Vietnam and vend our products to our customers at a fair price.

We started this business because we were all tired of wearing low quality, overpriced footwears and my team believed that there had to be a better way.

We are looking foward to opening up two more branches in Ikeja and partnering with Nike on a new line of international manufactured products.

I would personally invite you to stop by and try them on for yourself.
